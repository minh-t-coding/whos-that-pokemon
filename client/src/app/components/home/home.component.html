Who's that Pokemon?

How to Play

Scan the silhouette, guess fast, and collect every unique Pokémon. Wrong guesses cost tries and you can only skip five times per run—after that it's game over!

Scan

Type a name and press Scan to lock in your guess.

Skip

Jump to another Pokémon, but only five skips per game.

Hint Trail

Unlock cry, type, color, and reveal—but each hint lowers your score.

Catch

Guess correctly to add unique Pokémon to your collection.
